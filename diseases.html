Common Baby Diseases

Learn about common childhood diseases, how to detect them early, prevent them, and best care practices.

Disease Origin Signs & Symptoms How to Detect Prevention Care Tips
Chickenpox Varicella zoster virus Red itchy spots, fever, fatigue Appearance of rashes and fever Vaccination, avoid infected persons Keep skin clean, calm itching with creams, rest
Measles Measles virus Rash, cough, runny nose, fever Characteristic rash and symptoms Vaccination Hydration, rest, consult doctor if severe
Whooping Cough Bordetella pertussis bacteria Severe coughing, vomiting after cough Persistent coughing fits Vaccination, avoid exposure Keep baby hydrated, monitor breathing
Hand, Foot & Mouth Coxsackievirus Blisters on hands, feet, mouth, fever Blisters and fever observation Good hygiene, avoid infected kids Soft diet, hydration, clean skin
Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV) RSV virus Coughing, wheezing, trouble breathing Breathing difficulty, cold-like symptoms Avoid crowded areas, hand hygiene Consult doctor if breathing difficulty
